About Event

Smart Business Hackathon is an initiative sponsored by Technical Education Quality Improvement Program by Government of India (TEQIP-III) which will be jointly organized by JECRC Foundation and Rajasthan Technical University. Smart Business Hackathon is an innovative competition that builds up problem solving ability of individuals closely related to a business oriented environment. The competition involves assortion of young entrepreneurs into groups of 2-4 participants and creative handling of business cases.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the registration fee?

For RTU students,it's free & for non rtu students fees is 2000

What about accomodation and food?

Accomodation and food are absolutely free.

Will travel expenses be reimbursed?

Travel expenses will be bare by yourself.

What is the last date of registration?

The last date of registration is 31stJan 2019.

What can we build?

Proper Business idea with financial model,presentation & prototype.

Who can apply?

Everyone is heartly welcome.

What is the criteria for selection of a team?

Evaluation criteria will include novelty of the idea, complexity, clarity and details in the prescribed format.

What should be the size of a team?

A team can consist of 2 to 4 members.
